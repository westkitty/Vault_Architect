Output extras

What the formatter does

  • Extracts title, aliases, tags, metadata, summaries, and stat lines.
  • Builds a corpus registry, then links known entities and likely internal references.
  • Emits stable Markdown sections and YAML frontmatter for Obsidian and NotebookLM.

What it avoids

  • No network calls after load because there are no external dependencies to call.
  • No automatic linking of every capitalized phrase like a caffeinated goblin.
  • No hidden service dependency for ZIP generation.
